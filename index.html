Our Mission: A Guiding Light

The Firefly Initiative is founded on the belief that everyone deserves access to compassionate support and the resources necessary to make informed decisions about their own health and well-being. Our mission is to provide holistic harm reduction resource programs for safe supplies, resource education, and peer support to unhoused and low-income individuals facing substance use disorder. We support people in making their own choices with dignity and autonomy.

The firefly, a familiar symbol in North Georgia, represents hope, guidance, and resilience. We are committed to being a steady, compassionate source of support for our neighbors as they navigate their personal journeys and systemic challenges.

Respect

We honor the inherent worth and dignity of every individual, championing their autonomy and approaching all interactions with empathy and compassion.

Equity

We address systemic barriers and work to ensure that everyone has fair access to the resources they need to thrive.

Empowerment

We support individuals in building the skills and confidence to make self-directed choices about their own health and well-being.

Collaboration

We work alongside our community partners, stakeholders, and participants toward shared goals.

A New Chapter, The Same Commitment

While our name has changed from Jubilee Harm Reduction, our mission to provide compassionate care and resources remains our top priority. The transition to The Firefly Initiative is a positive step forward, built to strengthen our ability to serve the Athens community for years to come.

Our commitment to our neighbors and the people we serve is unwavering. You can expect a seamless process with no disruption to the vital resources and support you have come to rely on. We are evolving, not ending, and are dedicated to continuing to be a guiding light on the path toward self-directed choices and well-being.
